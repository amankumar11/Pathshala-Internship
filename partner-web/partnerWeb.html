PROFILE

Foreign Language Olympiad Association is a Pune, Maharashtra based organization established to promote the study of Foreign Languages in India. It is an initiative of Chanakya Legend Society.

With an indigenous syllabus and exam system, Foreign Language Olympiad Association aims to introduce children to the interesting world of Foreign Languages and the culture revolving around them.
